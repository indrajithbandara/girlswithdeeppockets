Girls clothes don't have pockets

Whether it's just the fashion machine making us look our best, or a conspiracy to make us own handbags, women's clothes just don't have enough pockets, and they definitely don't have adequately sized pockets!

Embrace the big pockets!

Let's celebrate clothes that have big pockets - send us a pic, a description, and ideally a link to where we can get it.
